# Day-to-day tasks for staff (instructors)

This page is for staff who sign in with a **work email** and mainly **view** information and submit **feedback**. If you are a **department head** or **college administrator**, you will have extra screens—see the guides for those roles.

---

## Before you start

1. Use your **college’s** APD link (see [Your college’s address and sign-in](../1-introduction/your-academy-and-address)).
2. Sign in on the **staff** sign-in page.
3. If you belong to more than one **department**, pick the right one when the system lets you switch.

## What you can usually do

- **See** courses, activities, students, and surveys your permissions allow (often read-only for instructors).
- Open **performance feedback** or similar views when you are listed as the **instructor** for an activity—so you can understand results shared with you.
- Send **feedback** through the feedback form when it is available (`feedbacks` routes).
- Read **announcements** your college shows to staff (`announcements#index`, dismiss on member).

## What you might not see

- Buttons to **create courses**, **enroll students**, or **edit surveys** usually belong to **department heads**, **quality leads**, or **administrators**.
- If you need access, ask your **department head** or **academy administrator**—do not share passwords.
