# APD Instructor Guide

**APD** helps staff view course and activity information and share feedback with your department. What you can click depends on your **role**—see [Who does what (roles)](../1-introduction/roles-in-apd).

Follow these steps to get started:

1. **Set up your account** → [Account Setup Guide](setup-account)
2. **Log in** → [Login Instructions](login-instructions)
3. **Daily use** → [Day-to-day tasks](day-to-day)
