# 👋 Welcome, Students

APD helps you submit course data and feedback efficiently. Use the **APD link for your college** (each college has its own address on the university’s deployment). If you are unsure, read [Your college’s address and sign-in](../1-introduction/your-academy-and-address).

Follow these steps:

1. **Set up your account** → [Account Setup Guide](setup-account)
2. **Log in** → [Login Instructions](login-instructions)
3. **Submit data** → [Quantitative/Survey Guides](quantitative-survey-guide)
4. **Telegram integration** → [Telegram Integration](telegram-integration)

🔹 **Need help?** Contact your college or department office first.
